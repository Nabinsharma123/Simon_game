Here's how to play:

1.Press the any button to start the game.

2.This will let Simon have his turn (Don't forget to remember which order he moves!)

3.When Simon is done, it's your turn.

4.Just match his moves in the exact order.

5.The game will keep track of button presses.

6.you loose if you did it correctly or if you lost.

7.Losing starts the game back at level 1.

8. Matching Simon's moves correctly advances you to the next level.
